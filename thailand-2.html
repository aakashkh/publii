Thailand Travel Itinerary: Jaipur to Krabi, Railay, and Phuket (May 2023)

In May 2023, I embarked on an unforgettable journey from Jaipur, India, to the stunning beaches and vibrant culture of Thailand. This six-day adventure took me through Krabi, Railay, and Phuket, filled with island hopping, local cuisine, and relaxation. Below is a detailed itinerary of my trip, complete with day-by-day highlights and a summary for quick reference. I'll add expense details later.

Trip Overview

  • Duration: 6 days (9th May 2023 – 15th May 2023)
  • Destinations: Krabi, Railay, Phuket
  • Travel Route:
    • Outbound: Jaipur → Bangkok → Krabi
    • Return: Phuket → Delhi → Jaipur
  • Visa: Obtained on arrival in Bangkok
  • Key Activities: Island hopping, exploring local markets, cultural shows, and beach relaxation
  • Cuisine: Breakfast buffets at hotels, local Thai dishes (Pad Thai, papaya salad, coconut ice cream), pizza, and plant-based meat burgers
